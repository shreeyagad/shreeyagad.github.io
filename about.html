About Me

Hi, I'm Shreeya.

I'm an incoming software engineer at Meta, working from the NYC office. In Dec. 2021, I graduated with a Master of Engineering degree in Computer Science from Cornell University, where I also attended as an undergraduate. I focused my studies on Machine Learning and worked under field experts Professor Kilian Weinberger and Professor Claire Cardie to implement applications powered by image manipulation and sentence classification algorithms.

During my undergraduate program, I was the Vice President of Community and an active member of Women in Computing at Cornell, a student organization dedicated to making computing inclusive for all. I was also a choreographer and dancer for Cornell's Bollywood fusion dance team, Cornell Sitara.
